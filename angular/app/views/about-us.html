About Us

The Nutrition Habit Challenge was created in 2010 by the co-founders of One on One, Bruce and Kym Burke. Its goal has been to inspire clients of One on One to make one or two small nutritional commitments using the "Less is More" approach. In 2014, it was opened up to the entire community. The event raised $10,000 for its charities with 720 people participating, including 100 families. In 2015, 2214 people participated, including 230 families!

The "Less is More" approach is founded on the premise that making small behavioral changes can stop a downward spiral of destructive behavior and begin an upward spiral of constructive behaviors. This upward spiral often inspires us to make other small modifications, resulting in meaningful dietary/lifestyle change that can significantly impact our health.

Enjoy the benefits of making a positive change in your diet/lifestyle, support either the Youth Service Bureau or Centre Volunteers in Medicine with a small optional donation and become eligible for some great prizes, including a trip for four to Disney World!

Special thanks to event sponsors Kish Bank, Otto’s of State College and One on One.
