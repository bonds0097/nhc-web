Welcome to the Nutrition Habit Challenge 2016!

Our Mission

To educate, motivate and inspire our community to take personal responsibility for improving their health habits.

Our Philosophy

Make a difference where you can make a difference. Start with yourself, then your family, then your community.

Top Four Reasons to Participate:

  1. Great way to positively impact your health.
  2. Great family activity! Demonstrate leadership and personal responsibility while providing education and having fun!
  3. Great opportunity to give back to your community by participating and promoting among your friends and co-workers.
  4. You might win a trip to Disney World!
