Welcome to the Nutrition Habit Challenge 2017!

What is the NHC?

The Nutrition Habit Challenge (NHC) is a community-wide wellness initiative designed to educate, motivate and inspire community members to take personal responsibility for improving their health habits. Using the "less is more" philosophy, the NHC encourages participants to make a manageable nutrition commitment for the month of February.


Participants create a secure login for the NHC site and then register for the challenge by selecting their healthy habit and tracking their progress during the month of February. This is all done through the interactive NHC website registration process and dashboard tracking interface.


Our hope is that you decide to not only participate, but become an advocate for the Challenge by encouraging your family and friends to do the same. Our philosophy is to make a difference where you can make a difference; start with yourself, then your family, then your community.

How does it work?

Here are the steps to participate:

  1. Select a nutritional behavior that you would like to change. Click here to see a list of available commitments.

    The behavior must be significant enough to positively impact your health, but manageable enough that you can be successful for 25 of the 28 days. LESS is MORE.


    Some ideas include:

    • No snacking on anything but fruit after dinner
    • Drink water instead of sode or juice
    • Prepare healthy snacks for work each day
  2. Go to the Registration page to create a Login ID and password to the secure NHC website.

    NOTE: If you use an email address for your login, you will need to verify your email before continuing to register for the Challenge. If you participated last year, go to Sign In in the upper right hand corner or click here to reset your password

  3. Register for the Challenge by signing in and completing the registration information on the registration page.
  4. Practice your nutritional commitment each day beginning Wednesday, 2/1/17 and edning Tuesday, 2/28/17, and track your success online through the NHC website.
